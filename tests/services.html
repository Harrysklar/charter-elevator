Our Services

Customized solutions for Maintenance, Repair, and Modernization to keep your elevators safe, reliable, and efficient.

MAINTENANCE

Customized contracts focusing on safety, reliability, and code compliance. The goal is to reduce downtime, extend equipment life, and enhance safety.

  • Tailored Maintenance Plans: Based on equipment type, usage, and environment.
  • Compliance: Ensure compliance with ASME A17.1 Safety Code standards. Includes inspection violation management and corrections.
  • Guaranteed Routine Service: Contracts specify number of visits per year. Cleaning, lubricating, and adjusting key components like doors, brakes, and ropes.
  • Flexible Contracts: From basic ‘Oil & Grease’ to comprehensive Full Maintenance, including emergency repairs.
  • Common Repairs: Door systems are the most frequently serviced due to heavy use.

REPAIR

Our approach emphasizes safety, fast response, and restoring full elevator functionality.

  • Clear and Comprehensive Scope: Providing clear pricing, honest recommendations, with accurate proposals and invoicing.
  • Flexible, Creative Solutions: Adapting to unique customer and equipment needs, fitting all budgets and timelines with customized, outside-the-box approaches.
  • Importance of Maintenance: While maintenance reduces the need for major fixes, repair services are ready when breakdowns occur.
  • Common Repairs: Doors are the most frequently repaired system – issues like door misalignment, sensor failures, and broken locks can cause elevator shutdowns.

MODERNIZATION

Customized elevator modernization solutions to upgrade aging and obsolete systems for improved safety, reliability, and efficiency. Emphasizes tailored planning, experienced project management, and transparent service without unnecessary upselling.

What’s Included:

Upgrades to controllers, door equipment, motors, safety systems, and cab interiors. Full or phased modernization based on budget and urgency.

When to Modernize:

Typical lifespan: 20–30 years depending on elevator type. Signs include frequent breakdowns, part availability issues, and extended downtimes.

Key Benefits:

  • Increased safety and energy efficiency
  • Fewer service calls and lower cost of ownership
  • Enhanced ride comfort and property value

Cost & Timeline Factors:

Varies by project scope, building type, number of stops, and regional labor rates. Duration ranges from 12 weeks to 6 months.
