Our Services

Customized solutions for Maintenance, Repair, and Modernization to keep your elevators safe, reliable, and efficient.

MAINTENANCE

Customized contracts focusing on safety, reliability, and code compliance. The goal is to reduce downtime, extend equipment life, and enhance safety.

  • Tailored Maintenance Plans: Based on equipment type, usage, and environment.
  • Compliance: Ensure compliance with ASME A17.1 Safety Code standards. Includes inspection violation management and corrections.
  • Guaranteed Routine Service: Contracts specify number of visits per year. Cleaning, lubricating, and adjusting key components like doors, brakes, and ropes.
  • Flexible Contracts: From basic ‘Oil & Grease’ to comprehensive Full Maintenance, including emergency repairs.
  • Common Repairs: Door systems are the most frequently serviced due to heavy use.

REPAIR

Our approach emphasizes safety, fast response, and restoring full elevator functionality.

  • Clear & Comprehensive Scope: Transparent pricing, honest recommendations, accurate proposals & invoicing.
  • Flexible, Creative Solutions: Tailored to equipment & budgets with outside-the-box approaches.
  • Importance of Maintenance: Prevents major fixes but repair services are always ready.
  • Common Repairs: Door misalignment, sensor failures, broken locks—fixed promptly to minimize downtime.

MODERNIZATION

Customized elevator modernization solutions to upgrade aging and obsolete systems for improved safety, reliability, and efficiency—without unnecessary upselling.

What’s Included:

Upgrades to controllers, door equipment, motors, safety systems, and cab interiors. Full or phased modernization based on budget & urgency.

When to Modernize:

Lifespan: 20–30 years. Signs: frequent breakdowns, part availability issues, extended downtimes.

Key Benefits:

  • Increased safety & energy efficiency
  • Fewer service calls & lower cost of ownership
  • Enhanced ride comfort & property value

Cost & Timeline Factors:

Varies by scope, building type, stops & regional labor rates. Typical duration: 12 weeks–6 months.
